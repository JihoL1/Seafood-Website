Seafood

Seafoods are food cooked using aquatic animals and plants such as fishes, shellfishes, crustaceans, seaweed.

Eating seafood provide benefits to us, for example, fishes are including omega-3 acid which support heart health, lowering blood pressure, reduces health risks with vitamin D and B2, which provide support on overall health, immunity and skin care.

Seafoods also provide high protein with good amount of fats.

About this website

In this website, there will be images and information of seafoods which are cooked in eight different methods from lots of different countries.

These methods are: steaming, frying, raw, grilling, baking, poaching, barbecuing and boiling.

Methods:

Steaming

Steaming is a method of cooking with steam. This method is done by putting food over the boiling water, boiling water continues to vaporize into steam and these steams are carrying heat to food over water to heat them up.

Benefits of this method are, it is able to avoid burning or overcooking foods. Steaming also loses less nutrients inside the foods, compared to other method using water to cook.

This method is also quick and does not require high cost for cooking while it does not require hard process for cooking.

Frying

Frying is a method of cooking with oil or fat. This method is done by putting food inside the boiling oil or fat and heating it until it is cooked.

This method has many variations such as stir-frying, pan frying, deep frying and air frying. Benefits of frying are being able to cook food fast while having good taste and textures.

Cooking in the oil or fat may cause lose of nutrients inside food and may cause problems in human body when consumed too much, these foods also have high calories which may lead to negative effects.

Raw

Raw is a method of eating food which are not cooked or has not taken any process.

Benefits of eating raw seafoods are it is able to avoid consuming toxins that are produced from heating the food and consuming more nutrients then other methods due to less lose of nutrients from cooking.

It requires care while eating these foods due to it being able to cause food poisoing and some other diseases that may cause from consuming raw seafoods.

Grilling

Grilling is a method of cooking with applying heat on surface of food. This method requires heat to applied directly on the surface of the food from the fire.

This method has some beneficial effects in nutrients, through being grilled, food may add some nutrients that are only able to be gained from grilling.

During process of cooking these foods, it may produce chemicals which are harmful for human body.

Baking

Baking is a method of cooking with dry heat, this method usually uses oven for cooking, but hot ashes or stones are also be able to use this method.

This method does not loses much nutrients inside food while making proteins easier to digest through the process of cooking and baked foods are mostly low in fat due to less oil use for cooking.

Baked foods have textures like fried foods but is more healthier compared to the fried foods.

Poaching

Poaching is a method of cooking by heating food while being in liquid such as water, milk, wine. This method cooks food in lower temperature compared to other method using liquid.

This method is good for preventing such food as egg or fish from falling apart due to boiling water.

Benefit of poaching is this method is good for food with low fat and high nutrients. This method also provides soft and juicy texture for foods.

Barbecuing

Barbecuing is a method of cooking by employing fire and smoke to cook food over the wood or charcoal. Grilling and barbecuing are pretty similar method of cooking but the difference is grilling is fast cooking while barbecuing is slow cooking.

This method is reducing fat of food this is because fats are dropped off while cooking with this method. This method also saves more nutrients within food compared to normal grilling.

Due to food being heated, it could produce chemicals that are capable of harming human body.

Boiling

Boiling is a method of cooking by putting foods inside boiling liquid such as water or milk. This method is simple and easy to do since it does not require much processes compared to other cooking methods.

It is also beneficial in nutrients because it improves and keeps nutrients inside food while destroying harmful sources that could harm human body.

Because of safety and benefits of it, this method is being widely used for cooking.
